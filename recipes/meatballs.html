Meatballs

Description

These are some really good meatballs that my mother taught me how to make

Ingredients

  • 1 pound meatloaf mix
  • 1/3 cup parmesan cheese
  • 1/2 bread cumbs
  • Salt, pepper, parsley

Steps

  1. Spray baking sheet with olive oil
  2. Mix all ingredients in bowl
  3. Shape the meatballs and spread them apart on the sheet
  4. Bake 350 degrees for 20 minutes
  5. Toss in sauce pot to finish cooking
